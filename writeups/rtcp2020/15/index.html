Writeup for 15

Category: Crypto
Author: Tom

15 was a challenge that was part of the 2020 Riceteacatpanda CTF. The challenge description consisted entirely of a long passage of ciphertext, without any hint.

I solved this by putting the ciphertext into this cipher type detector which revealed it's a monoalphabetic substitution cipher, which is fancy words for saying it swaps one letter with another, so S can become A, and D can become Z, etc.

I could then put the ciphertext into this brute force thingamabob, which rendered part of the plaintext sans symbols and proper capitalisation. By comparing the symbols and capitalisation to the ciphertext, I was able to work out the correct flag

PLAIN : rtcp{c4R3Ful_w1tH_3X1f_d4T4}
CIPHER: tovm{v4T3Ehq_f1oK_3J1e_i4O4}
