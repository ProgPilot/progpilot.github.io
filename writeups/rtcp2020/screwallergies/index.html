Writeup for Allergic College Application

Category: Forensics
Author: Eryk

This is a very simple flag:

  1. Download the .txt file
  2. Scroll down to the bottom
  3. Encase the last line into the rtcp{} format
The flag was: rtcp{_____} and you could tell this was the flag due to the the brackets and the underscores.
