Writeup for Robots. Yeah, I know, pretty obvious

Category: Web
Author: Tom

Robots, as it shall be known as from now on, was a challenge as part of the 2020 Riceteacatpanda CTF. The description seemed to emphasize robots a bit too, hinting that perhaps we should look at the robots.txt file for the CTF's domain.

When looking at this file, two directories are disallowed to robots - one called flag, and one called robot-nurses. Going into flag gets us rickrolled, and the flag can be found in robot-nurses.
