Writeup for Uwu?

Category: Web
Author: Tom

You can view a video version of this writeup here

Uwu? was a challenge that made up part of the 2020 Riceteacatpanda CTF. The challenge hint says that it gets harder the faster your internet is, and the other gives you this link.

When you visit that link, you get shown 6 pages full of uwus, each of which rapidly redirects to the next page in the sequence. In a couple of these pages, you can spot a little bit of text that's not uwu, which hints that the flag might just be sitting somewhere in the page content.

To retrieve each page, I used my history to make a list of URLs to grab, and made a quick script to download all these. I wrote another script to search for the flag and parse out all the plaintext (it was quite entertaining to read), which yielded the flag. Hurrah!
