Writeup for Zero

Category: Forensics
Author: Eryk

This challenge used a very interesting way of fingerprinting, called "Zero Width Spacers and Characters". After a couple of minutes of basic research (prompred by the name of the challenge) I stumbled across a Wikipedia article about this (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zero-width_space). I then stumbled across an online encoder/decoder (https://330k.github.io/misc_tools/unicode_steganography.html) which came very useful. The thing is that I started doing this challenge before the admins fixed the .txt file, so from the decoder I kept getting Asian letters and words. It was only after the admins announced that they've fixed the file that I've came back, re-decoded it and got the flag.

(My question is: how did 20 teams get the flag on the broken file?)

The flag was: "utflag{whyNOT@sc11_4927aajbqk14}"
