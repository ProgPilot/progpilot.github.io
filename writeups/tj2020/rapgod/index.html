Writeup for Rap God

Category: Forensics
Author: Eryk

As this was an audio file, the most logical thing to do was to examine the spectogram. I opened it in audacity, and I've noticed that at the very top of the file, it looks as if the tops of some symbols were visible. I had to find a way to see the rest of the symbols. I found this very useful website, which allowed me to alter the graph so that the symbols became visible. I then figured that there are actually 2 lines of symbols, and the second one way wayyyyy more difficult to read. After around 2 hours of altering the graph, I retrieved the symbols. I recognised them with massive amounts of time poured into playing around with Word when I was younger, it was the wingdings font. I then used the font to get the flag. Flag: tjctf{quicksonic}
